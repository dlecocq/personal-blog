The various dailies I read have all been covering a VW program called The Fun Theory</a>. It's a contest and entries are supposed to provide evidence that making things fun can influence peoples' behavior (presumably they mean to influence it positively).

For example, to encourage people to recycle bottles and cans more, a group turned a recycling receptacle into a "Whack-A-Mole" game of sorts.
